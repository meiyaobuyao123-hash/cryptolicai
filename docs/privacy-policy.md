# Privacy Policy — Crypto Earn

**Last Updated: April 3, 2026**

## Introduction

Crypto Earn ("we", "our", "the App") is a crypto yield data aggregation platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do **not** collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
- We do **not** store wallet addresses on our servers
- We do **not** track your financial transactions
- We do **not** use cookies or tracking pixels
- We do **not** sell any data to third parties

### Information Processed Locally
- **Wallet Connection**: When you connect a wallet via WalletConnect, the connection is handled directly between your device and your wallet app. We do not store your wallet address on any server.
- **App Preferences**: Language settings and display preferences are stored locally on your device only.

### Third-Party Data Sources
The App fetches publicly available market data from:
- **DefiLlama** (defillama.com) — DeFi protocol TVL and yield data
- **CoinGecko** (coingecko.com) — Cryptocurrency market data

These are read-only API calls. No personal data is sent to these services.

## Data Storage

All data is processed and stored locally on your device. We do not operate any backend servers that store user data.

## WalletConnect

Wallet connections use the WalletConnect protocol (provided by Reown). WalletConnect's privacy policy applies to the wallet connection process. We recommend reviewing their privacy policy at https://reown.com/privacy.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
